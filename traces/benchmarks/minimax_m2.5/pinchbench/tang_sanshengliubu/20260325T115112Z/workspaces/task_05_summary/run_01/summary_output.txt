Artificial intelligence has emerged as a transformative force in modern healthcare, revolutionizing how medical professionals diagnose diseases, develop treatment plans, and manage patient care through machine learning algorithms.

The key applications of AI in healthcare include medical imaging analysis, drug discovery, and predictive analytics. AI systems can detect diseases from medical images with accuracy matching experienced radiologists, accelerate drug development by analyzing molecular databases, and predict patient health risks to enable preventive care.

However, AI integration faces significant challenges including privacy concerns, algorithmic bias, and evolving regulatory frameworks. The future promises explainable AI and federated learning approaches that balance innovation with patient privacy and ethical considerations.
